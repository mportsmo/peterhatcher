Facilitator notes

The scenario

Peter has a pet turtle named Dribble (won at a birthday party) that lives in an open glass bowl on his desk. His 2.5-year-old brother Fudge keeps getting to Dribble, and Peter is desperate to protect him — but doesn't know what kind of solution he needs.

What trainees need to discover

  • Bowl has no lid; sits on a desk ~3 ft off the ground
  • Fudge climbs chairs to reach the desk
  • Bedroom door has no lock
  • Fudge has grabbed Dribble twice already
  • Bowl is ~8 inches wide, 6 inches tall
  • Small NYC apartment; parents won't spend much
  • Telling Fudge "no" and asking mom don't work

Debrief questions

  • What questions gave you the most useful information?
  • When did you feel ready to design? Were you actually ready?
  • What assumptions did you almost make without asking?
  • What constraints did you discover that would shape your solution?
